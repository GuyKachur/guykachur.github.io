About

Even though my legal name is Mark I have always gone by Guy, it just seems to fit me a lot more. I have always been a creative and fun individual and my name should reflect that. Currently I am prospective master’s student who has finally found my home in the tech world. It took me a while as my undergraduate degree was in human biology which in itself was gearing me up for med school. But while pursuing further application goodies I realized that the world of health wasn’t for me. So, I started a period of growth to figure out who I am and what I wanted to do as a career. Upon entering the master’s program at Northeastern University, at their Silicon Valley Campus , I was finally able to answer those questions. I am a developer.
