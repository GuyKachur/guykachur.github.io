About

Mark Kachur, or Guy as he's known by all but the most official of forms, is a prospective masters student who is finally at home in computer science. With an undergraduate degree in human biology and a minor in pyshcology guy was poised to take on medschool. When dipping his toes in the waters he decided that it wasnt for him. This started a time of discovery and growth in which he finally was able to find his home.

Upon entering the masters program at Northeastern University in their Silicon Valley Campus , Guy found his home among the programmers and developers. Starting with Racket and learning about functional programming helped him hit the ground running and allowed him to grasp large concept with little background. From then on it was class after class packed with new information and projects.

University of California Merced

UC Merced taught me a great deal but above all else it taught me how to think critically. While there I learned about the scientific method through my many classes and labs. Organic chemistry was the toughest but it allowed me to excel in biochemistry. Many of the classes I took delved deeply into how humans work, both from a biological standpoint as well as a pyschological perspective. Gaining experience within the office of student life and, in my senior year, representing my entire campus on an advisory panel to President Napolitano exposed me to many expereiences that no other campus would have given me. Proud to always be the unofficial mascot, The fighting fairy shrimp! or bobcat if youre official.
